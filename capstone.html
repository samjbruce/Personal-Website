Capstone Project

In the Pantry was my capstone project while at Actualize Coding Bootcamp. A user can add mainstay items from their pantry and search for and save recipes based on what they feel like cooking with. The app runs on a Rails backend and used Vue.js for the frontend. Throughout the process of making the app I learned many valuable skills including project management and reaching out to team members for help.
